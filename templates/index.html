Little Bobby Tables as a Service

Thanks to Little Bobby Tables as a Service, or LBTaaS, you'll never have to Google "little bobby tables xkcd sql injection funny" to find your favorite comic strip again!

The Problem

Little Bobby Tables, or Exploits of a Mom, is one of the most popular xkcd cartoons of all time. It constantly shows up in blog posts, technical presentations, funny emails to friends, and on printouts thumbtacked to cubicle farm walls in IT shops worldwide. But what happens when you want to share it or use it yourself? You have to stop and Google the damned thing, and if you Google the wrong terms, you may never find it!

The Solution

Finally, there's a solution to the "Googling that xkcd comic what was it called again?" problem. Enter Little Bobby Tables as a Service, a 100% RESTful, versioned API that makes consuming "Little Bobby Tables" easier than ever! All you have to do is make an HTTP GET request to http://lbtaas.jeremykendall.net/lbt?version=1, parse the JSON response body, and ROFL and share and be delighted to your hearts content.

Endpoints

LBTaaS likes to keep things simple, so there's just one endpoint. Make a GET request to /lbt?version=1 and, voila!, you have a JSON response body with all the info you need to enjoy "Little Bobby Tables".

Response Body

Here's what you can expect when you make your GET request.

{
   "status":"success",
   "data":{
      "name":"Exploits of a Mom",
      "permalink":"http:\/\/xkcd.com\/327\/",
      "image":"http:\/\/imgs.xkcd.com\/comics\/exploits_of_a_mom.png",
      "link":"\/lbt?version=1&format=json"
   }
}
